# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 3, 2026

## Introduction

Service Auto Manager ("the App") is designed to help users manage vehicle maintenance schedules, service history, and important vehicle documents.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App uses and how it is handled.

---

## Information We Collect

Service Auto Manager does **not collect, transmit, or sell personal information**.

The information you enter into the App, including:

- Vehicle information
- License plate numbers
- Mileage
- Service history
- Maintenance schedules
- Vehicle document dates
- User preferences

is stored locally on your device.

---

## Local Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device.

The developer has no access to your information.

---

## Backup and Restore

The App allows you to create backup files that are stored only in locations you choose.

Backup files are never uploaded to our servers.

---

## Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you about:

- Vehicle service intervals
- Timing belt replacement
- Vehicle document expiration dates

These notifications are generated entirely on your device.

---

## Internet Connection

Most features of the App work completely offline.

An internet connection is only required when downloading or updating the application through Google Play.

---

## Advertising

Service Auto Manager does **not display advertisements**.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App may rely on standard Android and Google Play services required for application distribution and updates.

No additional third-party analytics or tracking services are used.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be published on this page.
