Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Service Auto Manager is designed to help users manage vehicle maintenance, service schedules and important vehicle documents. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

Service Auto Manager does not collect, transmit or sell personal information.

Information entered into the application may include:

  • Vehicle information
  • License plate numbers
  • Mileage
  • Service history
  • Maintenance schedules
  • Vehicle document dates
  • User preferences

Local Storage

All information is stored locally on your device. The developer has no access to your data.

Backup

Backup files are created only when requested by the user and are stored only in the location selected by the user.

Notifications

The application uses local Android notifications to remind you about:

  • Vehicle service intervals
  • Timing belt replacement
  • Document expiration dates

Notifications are generated locally on your device.

Internet Connection

Most features work completely offline. An internet connection is required only for downloading or updating the application through Google Play.

Advertising

Service Auto Manager does not display advertisements.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be published on this page.
